NLP (Natural Language Processing)

NLP is a part of Artificial Intelligence which consists of Natural Language Understanding as well as Natural Language Generation.

It is the process of understanding as well as generating natural languageLanguage used by us to communicate so that human can interact with computers/devices in much more fluent and natural manner as if talking with each other.


Levels of NLP

  • Morphological Analysis
    • This level of analysis is concerned with the structure of words
    • It breaks every word in the sentence into Prefix morphemes that attach to front of word (un,non) , StemThe actual word , Suffix morphemes that attach to back of word (ness)
    • For example: The word unhappiness is divided into Prefix - un, Stem - happy, and Suffix - ness
  • Syntatic Analysis
    • This level of analysis is concerned with the formation of clauses and forming more meaning than a single word.
    • The goal of this process called Parsing is to convert the flat list of word that forms the sentences into a structure that defines the units that are represented by that flat list.
    • For example: boy to store go is rejected, and go to store or boy go to store, is accepted
  • Semantic Analysis
    • This level of analysis is concerned with the lexical meaning of the words
    • A single word can have many different meanings to it and is realted with other words used in the sentence.
    • By generating map of all the meaning a word can give and running through all the other words to see which meaning gives more sense is choosen.
    • For example: I will meet you at a diamond. In this sentence the word diamond has many meanings like shape, baseball field, etc, but the fact that `at` is present in the sentence makes the diamond either a time or place.
  • Discourse Integration
    • This level of analysis deals with the analysis of structure and meaning of text beyond sentences.
    • Some morphemes(mostly pronoun) are used to represent some other word in a compund sentences, and this Discourse integration level is where we analyiz them.
    • For example: I am playing football and it is a fun game which is my thoughts. The pronoun my represents I and it represents football.
  • Pragmatic Analysis
    • This level of analysis eals with the use of real-world knowledge and understanding of how this impacts the meaning of what is being communicated.
    • This enables for communication with systems and users possible.
    • It may use the user's history of queries and try to understand what the user wants to convey

Errors Encountered during NLP Process

  • Phonetic Error
    • The error due to poor cognition on part of listener
    • For example: Read or Reed, I scream or Icecream, etc
  • Typographic Error
    • The error due to typing mistakes
    • For example: Comptuer instead of computer
  • Orthographic Error
    • The error due to lack of comprehension of the concerned language
    • For example: Arithametic instead of Arithmetic
