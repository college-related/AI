Reasoning

The act of describing a concept with help of some knowledge base.
It refers to the process of concluding some output based on the knowledge base and the input provided.
A reasoning system is a system that generates conclusion from avialable knowledge using logical technique such as deduction and induction.


It is of two types:

  • Forward Reasoning
    • It works with a set of facts or rules towards a set of goals or conclusion. Also known as event-driven mode, antecedent mode, or data-driven mode.
    • The steps involved in forward reasoning are: Collecting rules or facts, recogonizing the rule that can succeed, conclusion is asserted to the working memory, iteratively find conclusion until solution is found.
    • Used in planning, monitoring,control,collecting symptoms to decide disease
  • Backward Reasoning
    • It works with a conclusion or goal towards the fact or rule it should have started. Also known as consequent mode, goal-driven mode, hypothesis driven mode.
    • The steps involved are: Derive hypothesis to the goal given, if the hypothesis is a fact we found the answer if not make it a sub-goal and do the same for those sub-goals until fact is found.
    • Used in diagnosis: from disease telling symptoms


Constraint Satisfaction

Constraint Satisfaction is the process of finding solution to a problem that imposes a pre condition or constraint which must be satisfied.

It consists of the following steps:

  • Constraint are discovered and propagated as far as possible throughout the system.
  • If no solution, search begins. A guess is made and added as new constraint which can be propagated and continue to finding solution.

Examples: Suduko, Graph coloring, N-Queen, etc

We have a initial constraint given we have to solve the problem by first propagating those constraint to the sub-problems and solution to those sub-problem is searched and finally the solution to problem is find.


Means End Analysis

M.E.A is a process of achieving a bigger goal by first dividing the bigger goal/problem into smaller sub-goals/sub-problems which have less complexity and easy to achieve and solving those sub-goals first then combining to get the final solution.
It consists of both forward and backward reasoning to help divide and conqure those sub-problems.

MEA relies on a set of rules that transforms one problem state into another.

The sub problems can be assigned priorities and higher priority can be solved first. The process of sub dividing the bigger problem into sub problem is called sub-goaling and the process of combining together the sub problems is called gluing.


For example: a company has to get turnover of 20 million dollars in this year. It can be sub-goaled into marketing, prodcution, campaining.
Which can be further divided by marketing (in social media, newspapers, commercials), prodcution (new product, product to go hand to hand with new product), etc

Now those smaller goals are completed in priority order which ultimately leads to the final turnover of 20 million dollars.
